Steampunk Slumber Party

Gears down. Goggles off. It's time to get cozy in the Foundry.

🌙 ✦ 🌙

About the Event

The furnaces are banked low. The Cogspire chimes have been set to a gentler hour. For a brief, beautiful window of time, the Brass Foundry trades its clattering ambition for something softer — blankets, candlelight, and the particular chaos that only happens when a bunch of creative souls decide to have a sleepover.

The Steampunk Slumber Party is the Foundry at its most unguarded. Bring your characters, your creativity, and your most elaborate fictional pyjamas.

Event Locations

Four cozy corners of the Foundry, opened up for the night.

Brass Bunks

Rows of brass-framed bunks sway gently on pulley chains. Patchwork quilts stitched with maps and constellations invite people to settle in. A metronome ticks softly like a heartbeat.

Copper Kitchen

Warm lamplight glints off copper pots and gear-driven kettles. The air smells faintly of cinnamon and coal smoke. A clockwork whisk stirs itself lazily in a mixing bowl.

Starlight Observatory

A vaulted attic of glass and brass, where telescopes peer into endless sky. Gears whir quietly as star maps shift across the dome. Candles flicker in lanterns, casting long shadows.

Ironclad Library

Towering shelves of leather-bound tomes stretch into the dim light, their spines glinting with brass inlays. Ladders on rails creak as they glide from stack to stack. Pneumatic tubes hiss softly, carrying secret notes between shelves.
