❄ ✦ ❄

About the Event

Winter has swept across the Brass Foundry in a roaring blizzard of invention, mischief, and mechanical cheer. For the first time in decades, the bitter cold has frozen the edges of the Rusted Swamp solid — transforming its murky marshes into a shimmering sheet of ice.
With this sudden shift, The Embermire Union (the winter aspect of the Rusted Swamp folk) has risen to prominence. They’ve claimed the frozen wetlands as their seasonal domain, adorning the swamps with lantern-ice sculptures, warm forges, and glowing fungal torches that burn like embers in the frost.
But not everyone is pleased.
From the ice-crusted waterways of the Skyrail supports, the ever-chaotic Otter Squad emerges — bundled in mismatched scarves, armed with gear-powered snowball flingers, and absolutely determined to reclaim their rightful winter playground. The Otter Squad has taken up arms and is ready for a snowball fight to the icy doom!
The Foundry is officially a Winterworks battleground — and you are stepping into the fray.

📋 Rules & Guidelines

Step 1: Choose your faction and join the fight.
Choose from The Embermire Union or The Otter Squad and prepare for battle.
Step 2: Create to earn snowballs for your faction!
Make Bots (30 points), Music (15 points), or Art (1 point) to contribute to the snowball stocks. Creating lore gives you 20 points for your team, and 5 points for every time you or someone uses it.
Step 3: Have fun!
Make silly, fun, wild contributions that showcase everything and anything you love about the winter, The Brass Foundry, and your friends.
