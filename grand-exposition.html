About the Event

Once a year, the Brass Foundry does something it almost never does: it shows off. The Grand Foundry Exposition is the city's premier showcase of invention, creativity, and carefully orchestrated magnificent chaos. Guild banners hang from the Cogspire. Inventor's Row doubles its stalls. The Skyrail Docks are polished — yes, actually polished — for the occasion.

Members of the Foundry are invited to bring their finest work: their bots, their music, their art, their stories, their most ambitious inventions and their most unhinged experiments. The Exposition is not a competition. It is a celebration — of everything the Foundry has built, and everyone who helped build it.

"The Exposition is not about being the best. It is about being the most fully, recklessly, magnificently yourself."

— Overheard at the Cogspire steps, attributed to no one in particular

Rules & Guidelines

No winners. No rankings.
Creations are exhibited, not judged.
Collaboration is encouraged and credited.
Works-in-progress are welcome.
Remixing allowed with attribution and permission.
The Exposition values process as much as outcome
